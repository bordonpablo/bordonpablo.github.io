Profile

Who I am

I'm a geophysicist and researcher with a focus on applied near-surface methods — ERT, EMI, GPR, and airborne radiometrics and magnetics. My work sits at the intersection of field acquisition, signal processing, and data-driven interpretation: I've spent a good part of my research applying supervised machine learning to detect subsurface features in GPR profiles, and I continue to develop processing workflows that make geophysical data more interpretable and reproducible. Currently based at Brandenburg University of Technology (BTU), I work on resource and environmental applications across a range of geophysical techniques.
